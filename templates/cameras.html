Dotnetix SafEye Nano cameras:

This page allows selecting cameras, editing camera detection zones and downloading incident logs and videos.

Edit camera detection zone:

  • Select a camera by clicking on the camera in the viewport.
  • Click on the camera view to update the detection zone. NOTE: detection zone takes 5 seconds to update.
  • To return to the camera selection viewport, click on Cameras in the Navigation Pane

Download camera incident logs and videos:

  • Select a camera by clicking on the camera in the viewport.
  • Click on the Open Camera button to open the camera interface.
